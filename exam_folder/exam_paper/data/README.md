# Data Files

## Root (exam_folder/data/)
- nih_terminations.csv: NIH grant terminations snapshot from Grant Witness (5,419 records, 57 columns). Contains terminated, frozen, unfrozen, and reinstated NIH awards, primarily during 2025.

## harvard_reports/
- Harvard amends lawsuit...pdf: Harvard Gazette article about Harvard pushing back against new funding cuts
- How progress happens...pdf: Harvard Gazette article about research progress

## Notes
Supplementary API-based datasets (OpenAlex, NIH RePORTER, USAspending, PubMed/iCite, ClinicalTrials.gov, BLS QCEW, PatentsView) are accessible via their respective APIs and will be queried as needed during the analysis.
